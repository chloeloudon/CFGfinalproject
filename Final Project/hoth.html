Hoth

Situated in the Outer Rim Territories, this remote, frozen planet is the perfect winter getaway for any adventurer or thrill-seeker. The vast icy plains and mountains offer unlimited play for a variety of snowsports, whilst those feeling more daring can explore old Rebel Alliance bases and glittering caves.


Points of interest

  • Ski or snowboard down the Clabburn Range

  • Explore the Rebel Alliance base Echo One

  • Ride across the icy plains on a native Tauntaun lizard

  • Trek through mysterious caves - but watch out for Wampas!
