Naboo

Sitting in the Southern regions of the galaxy in the Mid Rim Territories lies the beautiful planet of Naboo, famed for its unusual planetary core of waterways and tunnels and home to bountiful lush landscapes ranging from grassy plains to swampy lakes. Whether you're in the mood to mingle with the Gungans in the underwater captial of Otoh Gunga, or marvel at the classical beauty of the human capital of Theed, there's something for everyone.


Points of interest

  • Explore the treacherous Caves of Eleuabad

  • See the breathtaking waterfalls and wildflower meadows in the Lake Country

  • Soak up the arts and architecture of the floating city of Theed

  • Be in awe of the jewel-like bubble city of the native Gungans
