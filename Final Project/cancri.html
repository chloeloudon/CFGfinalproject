55 Cancri E

A planet for those looking to heat things up! 55 Cancri E sits outside our solar system (otherwise known as an exoplanet), and provides a whole new experience for space travellers. Whilst the planet itself is too hot to be habitable, our floating hotels above offer outstanding panoramic views of sparkling skies and swirling lava, the warmth shaking away any space chills.


Points of interest

  • Our 5 star Exo-solar floating hotels bring relaxation to the next level
  • Bask in the warmth of mesmerising lava oceans
  • Stare into the sparkling clouds that drift across the sky
  • Take a bubble-sphere trip across a sea like no other
