Mars

Mankind's second conquest in planetary expansion is Mars, a rocky red desert that has more to offer than meets the eye! Home to both the largest volcano and largest canyon in our Solar System, Mars has no shortage of natural wonder. Whether you take a ride in a Mars Rover across the vast deserts or spend your time learning in various research bases, there is something for everyone!


Points of interest

  • Climb the largest volcano in the Solar System, Olympus Mons

  • Speed across the martian deserts in a state-of-the-art Mars Rover

  • Get learning in a Mars Research Base and take part in a group archeological dig

  • Stay in luxury at the newest Grand Martian Hilton hotel or rent out a desert dome for a night under the stars!
