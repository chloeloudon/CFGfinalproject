The Moon

Looking for somewhere closer to home? Look no further than our very own Moon! Man's first visited planet in our Solar System, now an exciting holiday destination with plenty to do and see. Ideal for those wanting a short trip or a first time space experience - time to get lost in the history of space exploration!


Points of interest

  • Breathtaking views of Earth - not to be missed!

  • Visit the Moon's Space Exploration Centre and learn all about the Space Race and more

  • Dune Buggy racing...need we say more?

  • Float in zero G and glide through craters
